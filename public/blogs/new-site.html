Our new website!

May 8 Â· 2 min read

Our website has been the same for years now, long before we bought the bot and re-branded to Octave. One thing was very clear from the start, the SEO was terrible and the code hadn't been touched in ages.

Why we started fresh

One of the questions we've already received, is why not just update the past site? Modernize the code, and improve on the site rather than starting over completely. Well frankly, it was a mess. The dependencies had multiple vulnerabilities, the code was based in a dated language, the SEO opportunities were not abundant, and plenty more. While we do agree that the Discord mockup was interesting, it didn't help drive or maintain any traffic on the site.

We decided it would be much more beneficial in the long run to hire a designer for an entirely new site and start fresh. We're starting relatively simple with what was delivered by the designer: some HTML and CSS. Over time we're slowly going to rewrite it into something more dynamic, whether that be ReactJS or Vue we aren't sure yet.

Long term goals for the site

  • A functional dashboard to change bot settings
  • A status page to see when we're having issues
  • A visual music player within the dashboard
  • Sell premium perks on site, rather than only through patreon
  • Rewrite the site in an easier to deploy/develop stack
